# Black Pepper Benefits and Why It Pairs Well with Turmeric

Black pepper is more than seasoning. Its compound piperine can influence nutrient and compound absorption in some contexts.

## Why It Matters

- Improves flavor so meals need less added sodium.
- Pairs well with turmeric in curries and soups.
- Easy daily use in eggs, vegetables, and lentils.

## Pro Tip

Add freshly ground pepper at the end of cooking for stronger aroma.
