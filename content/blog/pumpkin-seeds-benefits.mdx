# Pumpkin Seeds Benefits for Minerals and Sleep Support

Pumpkin seeds are rich in magnesium, zinc, and healthy fats. They can strengthen nutrient quality in meals and snacks.

## Top Reasons To Add Them

- Supports zinc intake for immune health.
- Provides magnesium for muscle and nerve function.
- Works well in salads, soups, and yogurt bowls.

## Easy Habit

Keep roasted unsalted pumpkin seeds in a small jar and use 1 tablespoon in one meal daily.
