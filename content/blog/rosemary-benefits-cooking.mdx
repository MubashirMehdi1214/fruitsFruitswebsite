# Rosemary Benefits in Cooking and Brain-Friendly Meals

Rosemary adds aroma and bioactive compounds to meals. It helps reduce reliance on excess salt by improving flavor naturally.

## Practical Benefits

- Adds flavor without extra calories.
- Contains antioxidant compounds.
- Works with roasted vegetables and proteins.

## Kitchen Tip

Use fresh rosemary in soups and roasted trays; remove stems before serving.
