# Pregnancy Iron Foods: Safe Daily Choices for Better Intake

Iron needs increase during pregnancy. Food-first planning can help support energy and healthy maternal outcomes.

## Practical Food List

- Lean meats, lentils, beans, spinach.
- Pair iron foods with vitamin C sources.
- Avoid tea/coffee with iron-rich meals.

Always follow prenatal care guidance from your clinician.
