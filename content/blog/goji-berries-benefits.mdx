# Goji Berries Benefits and Smart Portion Guide

Goji berries are nutrient-dense dried berries used in teas, bowls, and snacks. They can support antioxidant intake when used in moderate portions.

## Why People Use Goji Berries

- Rich in carotenoids and antioxidants.
- Convenient shelf-stable snack option.
- Easy to pair with nuts or yogurt.

## Portion and Safety

Use 1-2 tablespoons at a time to avoid excess sugar from dried fruit. If you use medications, discuss interactions with your clinician.

## Quick Tip

Soak goji berries in warm water before adding to oats for softer texture.
