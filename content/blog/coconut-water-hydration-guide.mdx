# Coconut Water Hydration Guide: Benefits and Limits

Coconut water is a light electrolyte drink that can support hydration in hot weather or after mild activity.

## Benefits

- Contains potassium and fluid.
- Lower sugar than many soft drinks.
- Useful alternative to sugary beverages.

## Important Note

For intense training, electrolyte strategies may need higher sodium than coconut water provides.
