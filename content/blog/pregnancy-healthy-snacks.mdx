# Pregnancy Healthy Snacks: Simple Options for Daily Energy

Small balanced snacks can reduce long gaps between meals and help improve energy during pregnancy.

## Snack Ideas

- Fruit + yogurt
- Whole-grain toast + nut butter
- Boiled eggs + cucumber
- Roasted chickpeas + fruit

Choose snacks with protein and fiber for better fullness.
