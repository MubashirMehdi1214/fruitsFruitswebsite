# Brain Fog Foods Guide: What to Eat for Better Focus

Brain fog often improves when meal timing, hydration, and sleep-supportive nutrients become consistent.

## Focus-Friendly Habits

- Start the day with protein and produce.
- Include omega-3 sources several times weekly.
- Limit late-night high-sugar snacking.
- Stay hydrated and sleep 7-9 hours.
