# Foods for Anxiety Support: Nutrition Habits That Help Calm

Nutrition cannot replace therapy or clinical care, but it can support steadier mood and energy patterns when daily habits improve.

## Helpful Patterns

- Regular meals with protein and fiber.
- Magnesium-rich foods like seeds and leafy greens.
- Reduced high-sugar snack frequency.
- Better hydration across the day.
