# Almonds Daily: Benefits, Portions, and Best Timing

Almonds provide healthy fats, protein, fiber, and vitamin E. They are a practical snack for appetite control and cardiometabolic support.

## Benefits at a Glance

- Supports fullness between meals.
- Provides vitamin E and magnesium.
- Easy to combine with fruit for balanced snacks.

## How Much To Eat

Use about 20-24 almonds per day (roughly 1 ounce) based on your calorie goals.
