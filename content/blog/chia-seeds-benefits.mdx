# Chia Seeds Benefits: Fiber, Omega-3, and Daily Usage Guide

Chia seeds are among the easiest superfoods to include daily. They provide fiber, plant omega-3 fats, and minerals in a small serving.

## Key Benefits

- Supports bowel regularity through soluble fiber.
- Helps satiety and appetite control in balanced meals.
- Provides alpha-linolenic acid (ALA), a plant omega-3 fat.
- Adds texture and nutrition to yogurt, oats, and smoothies.

## Practical Daily Intake

Start with 1 tablespoon daily and increase to 2 tablespoons if well tolerated. Always consume with enough fluid.

## Simple Use Ideas

- Add to overnight oats.
- Mix with yogurt and fruit.
- Blend into smoothies.
